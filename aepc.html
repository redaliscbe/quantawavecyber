Advanced Executive Program in Cybersecurity (AEPC)

Industry-Integrated • Equipment-Based • Research-Oriented Executive Program

Program Overview

The Advanced Executive Program in Cybersecurity (AEPC) is a six-month, industry-integrated executive program designed to equip professionals with practical cybersecurity skills, emerging technology expertise, and applied research exposure. Delivered in hybrid mode (offline + online), the program emphasizes hands-on learning through real equipment and live devices, ensuring job-ready competence beyond theoretical instruction.

The curriculum is aligned with global technology leaders including :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}, :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1} and :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}, covering network security, SOC operations, cloud and endpoint security, digital forensics, cryptography, and AI-driven cybersecurity.

AEPC follows a learning-by-doing pedagogy using enterprise-grade security appliances, live SOC environments, cyber range simulations, capstone projects, industry internships, and guided research paper development.

Program Summary

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Program Fee: ₹64,000 + GST
  • Learning Mode: Hybrid (Offline + Online)
  • Weekly Effort: 6–8 hours
  • Nature: Executive | Industry-Integrated | Research-Oriented
  • Delivery: Instructor-led + Equipment Labs
  • Exposure: SOC, Cyber Range, Live Devices

Faculty & Mentorship

The program is delivered through a blended faculty model combining experienced industry professionals and academic experts. Industry practitioners lead specialized modules, hands-on labs, SOC workflows, and real-world case studies, ensuring enterprise-level operational exposure aligned with current industry practices.

Eligibility

  • Graduates / Final-year students in Computer Science, IT, Electronics or related disciplines
  • Working professionals in IT, Networking, Systems or Security roles
  • Basic knowledge of networking and operating systems is recommended

Industry Alignment & Certifications

Palo Alto Networks Certification: Cybersecurity fundamentals, Zero Trust architecture, and modern threat landscapes.

Cisco Systems Certification: Cybersecurity operations, SOC workflows, incident detection and response.

IBM Certification: Generative AI in cybersecurity, cryptography, and digital forensics.

Additional Exposure: Juniper, Check Point, Fortinet ecosystems.

Detailed Syllabus

  • Introduction to Cybersecurity

  • Cloud Security Concepts
