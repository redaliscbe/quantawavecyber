Cybersecurity R&D Lab

Quantawave Cybersecurity R&D Lab develops advanced defensive technologies through research in threat intelligence, malware analysis, DFIR, SOC automation and AI-driven cyber defense.

Request Lab Access View Research Areas
Threat Intelligence & Hunting

Multi-source telemetry fusion, IOC enrichment, adversary profiling, and proactive hunting strategies to identify emerging cyber threats ahead of time.

Malware & Ransomware Analysis

Static and dynamic analysis pipelines, reverse engineering, YARA rule development, and sandbox-based behavioural analysis for modern threat families.

Digital Forensics & Incident Response

Triage, evidence collection, timeline reconstruction, and incident reporting using forensic methodologies for secure recovery.

IDS/IPS Research & SOC Automation

Detection engineering, rule optimization, anomaly modeling, and automation pipelines that enhance SOC efficiency and reduce analyst workload.

Vulnerability Research & Ethical Hacking

Discovery and exploitation of security flaws in applications, networks, and hardware with responsible disclosure pathways.

AI-Driven Cyber Defense

ML-powered threat detection, anomaly scoring, adversarial analysis, and predictive defense models to automate cyber risk reduction.

Security Automation & SOAR

Automated playbooks, response orchestration, and real-time telemetry processing to accelerate incident response workflows.

OSINT & Threat Surface Monitoring

Automated open-source intelligence collection, asset discovery, breach detection, and attack surface analytics for proactive defense.

Secure Architecture & Zero Trust

Development of zero-trust frameworks, segmentation policies, and secure infrastructure blueprints for enterprise and cloud ecosystems.
