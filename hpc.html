High-Performance Computing (HPC) Lab

QuantawaveCyber HPC Lab delivers scalable, accelerated, and secure computing infrastructure for AI, analytics, scientific simulation, and next-generation research workloads.

HPC Cluster Infrastructure

Multi-node compute clusters with high-speed interconnects designed for parallel computation and advanced research applications.

GPU-Accelerated AI & Deep Learning

Optimized GPU environments for training and tuning deep learning models, enabling faster experimentation at scale.

High-Volume Data Processing

Infrastructure for massive parallel data analytics, ETL workflows, genomics research, and cryptographic data processing at scale.

Scientific & Engineering Simulations

HPC-powered simulation frameworks for physics, chemistry, CFD, FEM, climate models, and engineering computation pipelines.

Hybrid Cloud HPC

Elastic computing through integrated on-premise + cloud HPC, enabling workload bursting and containerized workflows.

Secure Compute Environment

Role-based access control, isolated compute nodes, encrypted pipelines, and compliance-ready environments for sensitive workloads.

Distributed Computing Frameworks

Support for MPI, Slurm, Ray, Spark, and other frameworks enabling efficient workload orchestration at scale.

HPC for Cybersecurity & Cryptanalysis

High-speed computation for encryption analysis, adversarial ML studies, and large-scale security model evaluation.

ML Model Training & Optimization

Automated pipelines for tuning, hyperparameter search, inference benchmarking, and deployment-grade model optimization.
