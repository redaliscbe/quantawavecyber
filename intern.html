Cyber Clinic Program

Our Cyber Clinic program is inspired by the international Cybersecurity Clinics model, where students provide supervised cybersecurity support to under-resourced organisations while building job-ready skills.

  • Work with QuantaWave-supported organisations such as small businesses, NGOs and educational institutions to improve their cybersecurity posture.
  • Carry out guided risk assessments, cyber hygiene checks, security awareness activities and basic configuration reviews under QuantaWave faculty / industry mentor supervision.
  • Build professional skills in scoping, documenting and presenting cybersecurity recommendations to non-technical stakeholders in a clear, actionable way.
How it fits into your internship
  • Primarily available to students opting for the Cybersecurity domain (limited intake per batch).
  • Clinic engagements can be mapped to AICTE internship / project requirements.
  • Team-based client projects with clear deliverables: assessment report, client briefing, and reflection note.
