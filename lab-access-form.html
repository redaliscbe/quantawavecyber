Lab & Testbed Access Request

Use this form to request access to QuantaWave Cyber R&D Lab facilities, including cybersecurity testbeds, quantum R&D setups, AI & cyber analytics lab, and HPC resources.

Internal & External Users Requests are reviewed and confirmed by email before access is granted.

Access Guidelines
  • Submit the form at least 3–5 working days before the requested date.
  • Bring your institute/company ID card on the day of access.
  • Use of datasets, tools and credentials must follow lab policies.
  • No copying of confidential or proprietary data from the testbed.

For urgent or special requirements, email director@quantawavecyber.com.
