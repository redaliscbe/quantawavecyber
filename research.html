Core Research Areas

QuantaWave Cyber R&D Lab focuses on convergent research across cybersecurity, quantum technologies, and high-performance systems.

AI-Driven Threat Detection

Machine- and deep-learning models for behavioral analytics, anomaly detection, automated triage, and reducing time-to-detect for ransomware, APTs, and insider threats.

Network & Cloud Security

Real-time traffic inspection, secure service mesh design, container/Kubernetes hardening, and secure cloud posture automation for enterprise deployments.

Cryptography & Post-Quantum Security

Research into quantum-resistant cryptographic algorithms, secure key management, and hybrid classical-quantum schemes to prepare systems for the post-quantum era.

Quantum-Secure Communications (QKD)

Prototyping and evaluation of Quantum Key Distribution systems, quantum-random-number generation, and hybrid secure channels combining QKD with classical networks.

Security Automation & SOAR

Automated playbooks, orchestration of detection-response workflows, and integration of telemetry pipelines to compress incident response loops.

Quantum Machine Learning (QML)

Exploratory research into quantum-enhanced models for pattern recognition, anomaly scoring, and optimization problems relevant to cybersecurity and signal processing.

Cyber Range & Testing

Realistic simulation environments for adversary emulation, red-team/blue-team exercises, and validation of defensive controls at scale.

Quantum-Resistant Blockchain Security

Research on post-quantum signatures, secure consensus mechanisms, and resilience of distributed ledgers against quantum-enabled attacks.

Cyber-Physical Systems & IoT Security

Securing smart sensors, autonomous systems, and industrial IoT through lightweight cryptography, secure firmware, and real-time anomaly detection.
